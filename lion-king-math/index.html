How to play
  • 🎮 Move with arrow keys / WASD, or on a phone/iPad just touch where you want to go — your lion walks right to your finger and stops there.
  • 🐢 Eat other animals (turtles, fish, gazelles) for points and to grow.
  • 🏷️ Every lion shows its level on a badge — green means you can beat it, red means it out-levels you. Your own level (★) is on Iris. Beat lions and answer math to level up!
  • 🥊 Fight other lions three ways: Scratch (J) is quick and hits all around you (same level or lower), Bite (K) is strong enough to beat lions up to 2 levels higher, and Jump-on (L) leaps at the nearest lion (up to 1 level higher). Run from much higher-level lions and hyenas!
  • ✨ Grab powers floating in the grass: 🛡️ Shield (a red bubble that protects you), 🔥 Roar (beat ANY lion and scare them away), 💨 Speed (run super fast).
  • 🧮 A math problem pops up with FOUR answer stones (1 right, 3 wrong). Eat the RIGHT one to LEVEL UP and grow big!
  • ❤️ Eating a wrong answer or getting caught costs a heart. Lose all hearts and it's game over.
  • 🌀 The edges wrap around — leave one side, appear on the other.
